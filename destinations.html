From the history of the samurai to the bespoke Japanese architecture this is the place to be, explore all of what Japan has to offer from rose gardens, museums and the ins out outs of day to day life in Japan.

You will get to explore Rome (mainly the ancient colosseum), the romantic canals of Venice and explore the difference both pizza and pasta have in the diffrence region of Italy


What are you waiting for, there's food on the line?!

Vietnam


Teaching in Vietnam opens up ever lasting beaches, food markets, and a plethora of ancient history, we just happen to offer the best internship for Vietnam in the industry that will let you explore and travel all of Vietnam and what it has to offer, and did we mention the food?
